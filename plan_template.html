Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my plan after purchase?

Your plan activates automatically within 2-5 minutes after successful payment. You'll receive a confirmation SMS with your login credentials and activation details.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade to a higher plan at any time. The unused balance from your current plan will be credited toward your new plan. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), all major debit and credit cards, and bank transfers through our secure Paystack payment gateway.

What happens if I run out of data before my plan expires?

You can purchase additional data bundles or upgrade to a larger plan. Your remaining validity period will be maintained, and the new data will be added to your account immediately.
