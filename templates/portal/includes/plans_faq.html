Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my plan after purchase?

After successful payment, you receive a voucher code by SMS when messaging is configured. Use it on the portal login page with your mobile number to connect.

What payment methods are accepted?

Mobile Money and cards (where your deployment enables Paystack).

What happens if I run out of data before my plan expires?

You can buy another package. How remaining time and data carry over depends on the package type.
